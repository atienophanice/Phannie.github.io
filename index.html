Girls lets fly

Girls lets fly is a project formed to support young girls and women to identify and use their full potential to empower them both economically and poliotically for them to take up laedership positions in different spaces

Vision Mission
A world where women can stand up and defend themselves To empower women conomically and socially

MOTIVE

Our main aim is:

Ending gender based violence to girls and women.

Empower women ecomnomically

Teaching women leadership skills

Help them start up their own small scale buissiness

Encourage education for both genders

Stop sexualization of girls and young women

Engaging men to help in figting GBV
